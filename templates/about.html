What is MedWheels ?

MedWheels an online platform for booking and tracking ambulances, offering real-time location updates, providing medical assistance during transport, and separate portals for patients and drivers, ensuring efficient and safe healthcare transport.

Our Mission

MedWheels aims to provide a seamless, reliable, and secure ambulance booking and tracking service that prioritizes patient safety and comfort. By offering tailored medical assistance, dedicated portals for drivers and patients, and robust data security, we aim to enhance emergency medical care and ensure peace of mind during critical moments.

"Your safety is our priority, from booking to arrival."

Our Vission

MedWheels looks forward to revolutionize emergency medical transport by becoming the leading platform for efficient, real-time ambulance services. We strive to set new standards in patient care, driver efficiency, and data security, ensuring that everyone has access to timely and compassionate medical support whenever and wherever it's needed.

How it Works

Step 1: Open MedWheels Website

Open Google and search for MedWheels ambulance service.

Step 2: Choose prefered Hospital

Choose yours prefered nearby hospital according to service availability.

Step 3: Choose your Ambulance

Choose your ambulance type(eg. MedBasic or MedPro) as per your requirement.

Step 4: Avail Ambulance Service

Get estimated distance and time of arrival from your location to destination , with real-time GPS tracking.
