Why join us as a driver?

Login and Set Availability: Log in and set your availability status on the app.

Receive Booking Requests: Get notified of new bookings with trip details and can accept or decline.

Route Guidance: MedWheels provides optimized routes with live traffic updates for faster navigation.

Status Updates: Update the current status (e.g., arrived, patient onboard) throughout the trip.

Trip Completion: After the trip, mark it as complete and receive feedback for performance evaluation.

Comission Structure

We take a 10% commission on every ride fare. This means that if the ride fare is Rs.10, we will take Rs.1 as our commission, and you will receive Rs.9.
